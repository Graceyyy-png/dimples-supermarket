As of today , the things I like are Wing Stop , Indian Food , And Century 25 . Wing Stop is my favorite place to get hot wings from . I always order the original hot wings boneless with fries and a drink . Sometimes I get ranch on the side . Indian Food . Oh my god ! I love Indian Food so much . Even if I wasn’t Indian I’d still love it . In my opinion , it’s the best spicy food ever .
I always either get butter chicken with rice and plain naan or shai paneer ( preparation of cottage cheese in a thick gravy made by heavy whipping cream , tomatoes , and indian spices ) with rice and plain naan . Indian Food is important to me because it’s something that is a part of my culture . Century 25 . I love to go watch movies there with my boyfriend or my friends . The rarest animal in the world is the vaquita (Phocoena sinus). It is a kind of critically endangered porpoise that only lives in the furthest north-western corner of the Gulf of California in Mexico. There are only 18 left in the world. A rare species is a group of organisms that are very uncommon, scarce, or infrequently encountered. This designation may be applied to either a plant or animal taxon, and is distinct from the term endangered or threatened.
